Welcome to Our Departments

At Crescent View, we're committed to providing exceptional medical care and compassionate service to our community. Each of our specialized departments is staffed by a team of dedicated professionals who are leaders in their fields. We pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art facilities and our commitment to the health and well-being of our patients.

From the cutting-edge treatments in our Cardiology Department to the comprehensive care in Oncology, and the innovative approaches in Neurology, we're here to ensure that you and your loved ones receive the best possible medical attention.

Our range of departments includes, but is not limited to:

  • Cardiology: Offering advanced heart care and treatment options.
  • Oncology: Providing comprehensive cancer care from diagnosis to treatment.
  • Neurology: Specializing in the treatment of neurological disorders.
  • Pediatrics: Dedicated to the health and happiness of children from infancy through adolescence.
  • Cancer Care: Expert care in cancer treatment, offering personalized therapies and compassionate support throughout each patient's journey.
  • ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat): Specialized in managing disorders of the ear, nose, and throat, ensuring comprehensive care for both adults and children.

We understand that visiting a hospital can be stressful, which is why we strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment. Whether you're coming in for a routine check-up or a specialized procedure, our goal is to ensure that your experience with us is as comfortable and stress-free as possible.

Explore our departments to learn more about how we can help you and your family live healthier, happier lives.

Our Hospitals

Crescent View is home to a cadre of experienced and compassionate professionals, steadfast in their commitment to providing exceptional healthcare and personalized attention across all services and departments.


Crescent View Medical Center

Location: Located in a suburban area, offering easy access from various parts of the city.

Specialties: Known for its outstanding cardiology and neurology departments.

Facilities:Equipped with advanced medical technology, including state-of-the-art MRI machines and robotic surgical equipment.

Size: A medium-sized hospital with around 300 beds, providing both inpatient and outpatient services.

Research and Education: Active in medical research, especially in cardiac health, and has a partnership with a nearby university medical school for training new doctors.

Crescent View Wellness Hospital

Location: Situated in a serene, natural setting, ideal for patients requiring long-term care or rehabilitation.

Specialties: Renowned for its comprehensive rehabilitation services and mental health care programs.

Facilities:Features a modern physical therapy center, a therapeutic pool, and extensive outdoor spaces designed for therapeutic activities.

Size: A smaller, specialized hospital with about 150 beds, focusing on personalized care and patient recovery.

Community Engagement: Known for its community health programs, including free health screenings and wellness workshops.
